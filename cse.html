About the Department

Dr. Pushpa B. Patil, HOD, CSE Department.

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) program was started in the year 1985 with an intake of 40 and has grown steadily. The present enhanced AICTE approved intake is 120, which runs a full time four year degree program. The main focus of the department is to produce graduates & post graduates with strong fundamentals in Computer Science and Engineering domain. The department has a total of 24 teaching faculty members with various specializations, out of which 4 are PhD holders and 10 are pursuing PhD. Faculties in the department have outstanding teaching experience. The department has excellent infrastructure with state of the art equipment and software tools. The department is spread over the sufficient area and housed with six well equipped laboratories viz. Best Practices
Bridge course on C, C++ and Data Structure

Every year, beginning of the semester one week bridge course on C, C++ and data structures will be conducted for 3rd and 4th semester students with practical approach.

Success Gatha

Success Gatha will be conducted to wish the placed students and to share their success journey with pre-final year students. This event helps to encourage and motivate the pre-final year students to prepare for the campus drives of reputed companies.

Appreciation for the faculty for 100% result

This practice helps to encourage all the faculty members to improve the student academic performance.
